1. Introduction

At ninekiwi, we are strictly committed to securing your digital footprints and personal nodes. This document details our architecture for collecting, handling, and isolating your data from unauthorized processes.

2. Information Collection

  • Account Telemetry: Name, contact vectors, organizational role.
  • Network Data: IP pathways, browser metadata, operating architecture.
  • Operational Data: Interactions with our digital interfaces, collaborative models, and uploaded files.

3. How We Use Information

Your extracted data operates exclusively to sustain, debug, and elevate the ninekiwi platform. It is processed to complete service initialization, optimize API exchanges, and strictly enforce our operational guidelines.

4. Data Protection

Our infrastructure executes end-to-end encryption and complies with deep enterprise security checks. All payload data is wrapped with AES-256 standards while at rest, ensuring a pristine isolation from the external environment.

5. Fraud Prevention

We actively run Advanced Fraud Detection to sanitize input nodes and intercept illicit network vectors. Furthermore, our payment systems strictly adhere to being PCI DSS Compliant, halting anomalies before they propagate.

6. Data Sharing

We orchestrate strict non-disclosure principles. We deploy your data to third-party endpoints only when absolutely critical for backend operations (such as Razorpay for secure handshakes) or when demanded by absolute legal frameworks.

8. Your Rights

You command ultimate authority over your digital proxy. Rights include accessing data, demanding rectification, or triggering account erasure. This includes rights for Restriction & Objection, allowing you to request restriction or object to processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@ninekiwi.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days, in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Data Retention

Data blocks remain active only within necessary operational windows. Post-termination, core traces are systematically flushed, maintaining brief archival periods strictly for auditing protocols.

10. International Transfers

Global interactions inherently require cross-border data packet routing. We bind international server hubs to strict data equivalency agreements matching primary security tiers.

11. Children's Privacy

Our advanced tools are restricted entirely from individuals under the operational age of 18. Accidental data captures from underaged nodes are purged upon detection.

12. Policy Changes

Algorithms evolve. We dynamically update this Privacy Architecture based on technical or legal shifts, reflecting the new system timestamps on this page.
